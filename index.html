Desk Research Methods

Posted on 2021-02-03

[TOC]

​

​ Due to the impact of Covid-19 this year, there are certain restrictions on the way we conduct research, so many people use desktop research methods for academic research.

What is desk research?

Desk research involves reanalysis, interpretation or review past data. The role of the researcher is always to specify how past data informs his or her current research. Compared with first-hand research, desktop research is easier, especially because researchers are less involved in the actual data collection process.

Steps

​ Since this holiday I will carry out a desktop study on the street vendor economy, the following step-by-step instructions for the desk study also initially show the general steps and logic of my research, so I will also use the street vendor economy as an example to illustrate

Step 1 Present your research questions

​ First, determine the general topic, and then read through the existing papers to find out whether the direction I determine can fill the gaps in the existing academic research, and on this basis, determine the specific research direction.

Step 2 Find secondary data sets

​ Most research begins by specifying what is known and what seems to be missing on the subject. This process involves considering the types of data previously collected for the topic. At this point, after reviewing the literature and specifying the research questions, a decision can be made rely on secondary data. You can do this if you find that past data can be fully reused in your own research.

​ But how can you find past data useful for your research? You can do this by looking at literature related to topics of interest to you. In this process, you will identify other researchers, organizations, institutions, or research centers that have explored your research topic.

​ Most importantly, be sure to get the permission of the original author before using the data.

Step 3 Evaluate secondary data sets

​ Because the data we collected may have some defects, such as lack of reliability or not consistent with our research topic, we need to evaluate these collected data and data sets.

​ You could ask yourself several questions to conduct this process, for example:

  1. What is the purpose of original research, whether it match your chosen topic?

     2.  Who, when, and how did the data collected, whether it is reliable?

Step 4 Prepare and analyze auxiliary data

After going through all those process above, you need to start preparing and analyzing. This depends on whether the research is qualitative or quantitative. Personally, I would conduct the street vendor economy research qualitatively first, after that, I would start to collect my primary data based on the qualitative outcome, and then start the quantitative research.

Investment skills

​ First of all, I learnt many techniques which could help analyze the stock trend, company and the whole industry, and try to apply those into the final report writing, such as, SWOT analysis, PESTLE analysis, Five Force Model and so on. Though these are simple and basic techniques, it is still a challenge for us to use them in real analysis rather than exam paper.

​ Apart from that, we attempted to use mathematical methods to predict the trends. Here is one example of our work.

​


Considering the huge difference between an industry giant and small entrepreneurial enterprise just finished Angel round, we use several mathematic skills, basically linear regression, to track the Beyond meat data back to 2010 the value of time. Then, to make the prediction actually useful, we make an a little bit more complex refixation on the parameters for giving a useful prediction. The basic principles, is based on a thought, that we have to realize, different from Beyond Meat, Meatable is a truly Biotechnology Company, that means the developing cost will be much higher, and their company growth will appear a distinct platform, It is also special that, Meatable’s products are food, they hope their products are at normal price, they will not start being cheap, but will finally become necessities in economic. We assume, Meatable will put their first product in volume production in 3 years, and then the second and followings will be much quicker, which is actually a relatively optimistic assumption.

So in the final prediction, we see their net profit and EBITA will not be beautiful in 5 years, because I don’t believe they will low the price successfully such soon, neither will the food crisis come so soon, yet you may tell that the EBITA margin will grow. They will have a tough financing target in three years, to have this prediction we also refer to Impossible food and Melphis Meat. We believe the mean source of funds will still comes from VC companies and personal investors, there may be other investment or even donations from government, organizations or people.

The funds will mostly be used on the development of products firstly, then they will cost a lot on purchasing cell reactors for volume production, they will also need money on marketing in the future, but that may be not so soon.

​ Beyond that, we learnt to read, understand and analyze reports of different companies, such as balance sheet and cash flow statement, which needs much basic knowledge of accounting and finance.

Team work

​ Basically, this is a team work. However, go through the three-month period, I found that we didn’t do a great job on team collaboration.

​ First of all, we randomly assigned different sections to the members and then conduct separate research. This was really unreasonable. For stock research, everyone has their own different parts they interested or good at, and, the most important is, we should conduct ONE portfolio for our customer based on the case study, and this is about portfolio management.

​ Additionally, as the leader of the group, I didn’t make great connection with every member. It is really important for a leader to get members of the group keep close contact. How to organize a group discussion effectively is what I need to think about.

How to improve

​ Firstly, the learning of professional knowledge and skills is the most basic and most important. According to the two reports we have written, what is more important is the lack of professionalism and the lack of investment thinking, which is also the most important point of the organizer. The cultivation of investment thinking can not be formed in a day or two, which requires me to read a lot, absorb a lot of different information, and constantly think and reflect.

​ As for the management of the organization, I need to reasonably arrange tasks according to the characteristics and abilities of each person. For leaders, it is more necessary to have a general control and planning for the long-term direction of the whole team.

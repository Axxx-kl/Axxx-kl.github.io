Who am I

Posted on 2021-02-03

​ I am Alexis from NFLS. I may a little serious at first sight, but after getting to know me well, you will discover a completely different me.

​ Many of my classmates who worked with me on projects consistently evaluated me as a very logical and organized person, which is absolutely true. I am very proud of having the ability to arrange and plan things reasonably well. Even though you might think that it is not a big thing, many people cannot do it. I set weekly, monthly and annual goals for myself, and even set objectives for different life stages in the long term future. In order to achieve my final goal, I am used to adjusting my plan and reflecting on it frequently. Such a habit makes me feel that what I am doing every moment is meaningful.

​ If you don’t even know the target, what is the point of practicing bow drawing every day? I adopt the same approach to studying as well, which greatly helps my time management skills. In addition, I am accustomed to making my projects, speeches or articles look very logical and structured.

​ All of the above shows that I am capable of managing a team. Apart from that, what I particularly enjoy is observing my surroundings and analyzing people’s characteristics people. This not only enables me to develop friendly relationships with most people due to my strong people skills, but also to excel at identifying others’ talent, which is also necessary for a team manager.

​ However, in my opinion, there are still a lot of skills I need to improve, for example, I wish to be more creative and adept at academic analyzing. Anyway, still a long way to go.

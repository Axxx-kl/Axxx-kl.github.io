Xiaomi Corporation - MNC

Posted on 2021-02-04

​

[TOC]

When we were learning about multinational companies in our business class, I did a little survey about Xiaomi Corporation in order to learn how can MNC affect the host countries.

Introduction

Xiaomi (/ˈʃaʊmiː/ ) Corporation is a Chinese multinational electronics company founded in April 2010 .

The fourth company in the world after Apple, Samsung and Huawei to have self-developed mobile system-on-chip (SoC) capabilities. ( From Wiki)

What does Xiaomi offer?

Where is Xiaomi？

  1. China - headquarter

  2. Singapore, Japan, South Korea, Russia, South Africa and most countries and regions in Southeast Asia and Europe……

Positive impact

Smartphone market

Methods of sales

This kind of online direct sales that Xiaomi first started, saved the high cost of channels (of course, it also went through some channels later, operators, e-commerce and traditional channels are less talked about), which has caused many mobile phones and manufacturers follow the example of online marketing.

Enable more people to use smartphones

The very low price of Xiaomi’s phones has allowed it to quickly gain market share and allow more people to use smartphones. This increases the market competition

It is precisely because of the increased demand for Xiaomi mobile phones around the world, which has offered employment opportunities people around the world. Help the local government to gain more tax revenue, and increase the local GDP there.

Technology

IOT(Internet of Things)

Xiaomi formed a more complete smart home ecological chain, most of its products are used by many people. Such a more mature Internet of Things environment has greatly changed the our ways of life, making our life more colorful and more convenient.

Xiaomi’s IOT planet!!

Xiaomi Vela is a new IoT software platform that can run on a variety of chip platforms, especially IoT devices. Therefore, it can connect various MCU devices, software, communication protocols. The company’s primary goal is to support all scenarios and build infrastructure for the IoT industry.

https://iot.mi.com/vela (Click here to find out more)

5G

Xiaomi said its roll-out strategy of 5G smartphones in 2020 remains unchanged, and it expects at least 10 new 5G smartphone models to hit the market this year.

It will also boost 5g construction in China and around the world.

CSR

To combat the global epidemic, Xiaomi has provided necessary materials and assistance to its employees and supply-chain companies, while donating supplies to many affected areas around the world.

Tech & Knowledge transfer

Vision – Innovation for everyone
Xiaomi’s belief and dedication in innovation, together with the support of Mi fans, the driving forces behind our unique Mi products.

For example, Xiaomi Vela is already available to developers in a small preview of its features, offering free access to chip vendors and companies related to the IoT sector.
Xiaomi provides many creative products for people throughout the world.

Negative impact

Low price

Xiaomi’s mobile phone profits are suppressed low, mainly because they want to compete for the market share of low-end mobile phones through higher cost performance. However, this will cause unhealthy price competition in the market. Not only that, this is likely to result in companies not having enough funds for research and development.

How much Xiaomi’s phones are?

The average selling price (ASP) of Chinese electronics company Xiaomi’s smartphones was around 980 yuan in 2019, which was about 137 U.S. dollars. The smartphone segment is Xiaomi’s largest business segment, accounting for around 60 percent of the company’s total revenue in 2020.

Negative impacts on users

The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology of China collectively interviewed 18 mobile resale companies, including Xiaomi Technology, in response to the problem of serious nuisance of spam from some mobile resale companies in 2019.

The Cyber Security Administration of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology interviewed Xiaomi in 2020, because Xiaomi has some problems such as suspected fraudulent calls and a substantial increase in the number of fraudulent SMS reports, which has caused adverse effects on users.

Analysis of Tencent's CSR

Posted on 2021-02-08

When learning corporate social responsibility, I did a small research on Tencent’s CSR, and here is what I found.

Poverty alleviation

​ Tencent is actively exploring “Internet + poverty alleviation”. Its Tencent public welfare platform and Tencent Weicun platform are the two most typical platforms.

​ Based on the characteristics of the Internet, Tencent’s public welfare platform extensively mobilizes poverty alleviation forces. As of the end of 2018, the number of charity projects raised through Tencent’s charity platform exceeded 50,000.

​ Tencent has worked with many places to promote the “Tencent Smart Campus” education and poverty alleviation project, to help poor students play the road of life development, on the other hand, Tencent and the Beijing Hofeng Art Foundation jointly launched the “Art Action” public welfare project, jointly explore the “Internet and Art Education” to provide rural children with artistic enlightenment. Help deal with Covid-19

​ During the covid-19, Tencent conferences were open to users free of charge, providing telecommuting for enterprises and providing remote consultation for medical institutions.

​ Tencent Cloud’s SMS service, which covers more than 1,000 operators in more than 200 countries and regions around the world, can help government departments quickly pass on epidemic prevention information to the public.

​ Tencent also set up an online platform to provide the public for real-time data about the global Covid-19.

​ Tencent set up the Global War Epidemic Fund in March this year to procure and donate important medical supplies such as masks, ventilators and protective equipment to the hardest-hit areas of the new crown outbreak.

​ Tencent cooperated with FC Barcelona Foundation to deliver personal protective equipment to FC Barcelona. The protective materials are distributed to Catalan medical staff through the club foundation network

( The Barcelona Foundation received the first batch of protective clothing on April 12 )

Environmental protection

​ Tencent and China Environmental Monitoring Station jointly launched the “Air Quality” query service to help the public get the most authoritative urban environmental air quality data released by the authorities in the first place. At the same time, the two sides will also carry out in-depth cooperation in the construction of environmental monitoring cloud platform, promote the construction of environmental supervision and information technology.

Inheriting traditional Chinese culture

​ Tencent has launched a number of projects to spread traditional Chinese culture in China and even around the world through digital technology. For example, since 2016, Tencent and Great Wall have formally cooperated to inherit and promote the spirit of the Great Wall from multiple levels such as charity fundraising, image incubation, and cultural education, so that the Great Wall culture will regain its charm.

​ Among them, the “Protect the Great Wall, Add One” public fundraising project, as of December 31, 2018, attracted nearly 400,000 people to participate in the Great Wall Protection Fundraiser, becoming the cultural heritage public welfare project with the largest number of fundraising and participation on Tencent’s public welfare platform.
